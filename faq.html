FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


PRICING AND COMMISSIONS

All currency is in USD. Prices generally range from $15-$75, depending on the complexity of the piece and the cost of materials. Comms are currently closed.


SHIPPING AND LOCAL PURCHASES

I ship locally and nationally, for an additional $5. International shipping is available, but please inquire about price estimates for shipping to your country beforehand.If you are located locally and would like to meet in-person to get your piece, I am more than happy to arrange that. Please DM me on Instagram or send me an e-mail for more details.


WHAT ARE THEY MADE OF?

The bones (jumprings, pins, wire...) are all made of stainless steel, some plated with gold or silver. My older pieces are made with brass, a cheaper and less sturdy material. The beads I use are usually made of glass, but I tend to use freshwater pearls and metal beads/bead caps on occasion. I also use chains and spikes for added detail. I typically don't know what the metal of these are made of (though it's usually brass), so if there's any concern with allergies or rusting be aware!


WHERE DO YOU GET YOUR MATERIALS? HOW DO YOU MAKE THEM?

My favorite place to shop for beads is Bead Boys, a local shop with a wide selection of beads including glass, locally sourced pearls, and seed beads. Aside from that, Etsy is a great source for absolutely everything.

As far as assembly, there are TONS of videos (especially TikToks) explaining visually and in-depth how to create a jewelry piece. Simply searching #handemadejewelry or #jewelrytutorial brings up lots of helpful content.
