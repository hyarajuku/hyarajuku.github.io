FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

pricing and commissions

Prices generally range from $15-$75, depending on the complexity of the piece and the cost of materials.

what are they made of?

The bones (jumprings, pins, wire...) are all made of stainless steel, some plated with gold or silver. the beads i use are usually made of glass, but i tend to use freshwater pearls and metal beads/bead caps on occasion. i also use chains and spikes for added detail. i typically don't know what the metal of these are made of, so if there's any concern with allergies or rusting please be aware!

where do you get your materials? how do you make them?

My favorite place to shop for beads is Bead Boys, a local shop with a wide selection of beads including glass, locally sourced pearls, and seed beads. Aside from that, Etsy is a great source for absolutely everything.

to put all the pieces together, you will need pliers (and i recommend a one-step looper to make things easier)
