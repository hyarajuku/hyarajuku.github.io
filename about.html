About HYARAJUKU

HYARAJUKU is the stagename/business run by Mimi E. (me!)

The name came to be because of Mimi's lifelong admiration and appreciation for Harajuku, a neighborhood in Japan, known for its eccentric fashion and acceptance of expression through it. HYARAJUKU takes heavy inspiration from the styles of the neighborhood, including gothic lolita, a fashion subculture with influence from Victorian-era gothic fashion.

A few other favorite sources of inspiration include: FRUiTS magazine, bunnies, antiques, Cocteau Twins, crosses, lace, and trad goth fashion.
